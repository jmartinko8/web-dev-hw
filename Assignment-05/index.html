Breakfast Burrito

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 1 flour tortilla
  • 1 tespoon butter
  • quarter cup shredded mexican cheese
  • table spoon Alpine Touch seasoning
  • 3 slices of ham, or bacon, or sausages

Directions

  1. crack 4 eggs in a bowl and scramble them
  2. turn on stove to 7 out of ten, melt butter in pan
  3. once melted, place scrambled eggs in pan
  4. cook for 5-10 minutes will moving eggs around in pan
  5. when eggs are done, place tortilla in microwave for 15 seconds
  6. when tortilla is finished, remove it from microwave
  7. put eggs in straight line across middle of tortilla
  8. pour cheese evenly throughout the eggs
  9. cut up ham, then add on top of eggs
  10. sprinkle alpine touch
  11. wrap up tortilla
  12. enjoy
