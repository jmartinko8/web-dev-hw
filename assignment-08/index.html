How to Bunt a Baseball

directions

  • 1 Baseball Bat
  • 1 baseball
  • 1 friend to throw you the ball
  1. take the bat and get into a batting stance based off of your dominant hand (ex. right handed)
  2. have your friend stand about 20-25 feet from you
  3. turn your shoulders and bend your knees
  4. present the bat out in front of your body, at about a 70-degree angle
  5. as your friend throws the ball to you, move your body up or down to adjust your bat to where the ball is pitched
  6. let the ball hit your bat, (dont stab at the ball with your bat as it comes toward you, use your bat to deaden the ball)
  7. if your partner throws it too high or too low, pull your bat back towards your shoulder, and try again
  8. as you practice it, try angling your bat towards the first and third base lines. directional bunting is very important in trying to master this skill.
