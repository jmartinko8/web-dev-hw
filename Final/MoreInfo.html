General Overview

The University of Montana Club Baseaball team is a school club. All members are students of the university and have a GPA of at least 2.0 and are enrolled in at least 12 credits. the club team is memebers of the NCBA North Pacific (NOPAC) East Conference. Seasons are broken up into Fall and spring, with the fall consisting of non-conference games and spring consisting of our teams conference schedule.

Teams in Conference

  • Idaho State
  • Weber State
  • Utah State
  • Montana State
