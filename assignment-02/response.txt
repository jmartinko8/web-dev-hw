6. How does information travel through the internet?

  - Information travels through the internet in bits, which is binary information, 1 meaning on, 0 meaning off.
  these bits travel with the use of electricity one way it can travel is through fiber cables that allow the
  bits to travel at the speed of light from one source to another. the bits bounce up and down through the cables,
  so, depending on the angle, bits can travel at different speeds.
